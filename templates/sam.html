Position Groups Salary Analysis

(2011 - 2019)

We decided to go deeper than just surface level team salary analysis. One such analysis is looking at how teams allocate their resources between pitchers and position players. The graphs below look at the percentage of each teamâ€™s payroll dedicated to pitchers and position players from 2011-2019 and how that correlates to statistics such as ERA, WAR (Wins Above Replacement), and Wins.

The first set of graphs put the payroll percentages into bins and display the average ERA and WAR for each team within each bin. The next two graphs show in order from highest to lowest percentages of payroll each team has spent over the timeframe 2011-2019. The last two look at payroll percentages for each season, sorted by team, and with different colors representing their win percentages.
